Understand Your Koi Like Never Before

A mobile application developed to enhance the care and management of Koi fish by providing real-time identification of diseases and growth stages. This application leverages advanced image processing and machine learning technologies to analyze high-quality video footage of Koi fish captured in various aquatic environments. Simply KOILY can instantly track growth, diagnoses diseases with just a snap.


Research Project Scope

Research Problems


Difficult to identify diseases.

Difficult to identify diseases severity.

Difficult to identify growth stage.

Research Objectives


To develop a comprehensive mobile-based Koi fish detection , disease and growth progress identification system using an image and video processing, aiming to enhance the efficiency and accuracy of identifying Koi fish growth stage and detecting diseases in real-time..

Methodology

Feachers

Capturing Quality Video

KOILY starts by ensuring that high-quality video of Koi fish is captured under various conditions. This feature able to adjust for factors like light refraction and movement of water, which are crucial for obtaining clear and usable video frames that form the basis for further analysis.

Growth Identification

KOILY assesses the growth stages of Koi fish by analyzing changes in size, shape, and coloration. This information helps users understand the developmental phase of their fish, tailor care and feeding regimes, and monitor overall well-being.

Disease identification

The application then analyzes the video frames to identify potential diseases. By using machine learning models, specifically trained on patterns and symptoms of common Koi diseases, KOILY can detect and alert users to the presence of health issues in real time. This allows for immediate action to treat affected fish, preventing disease spread and promoting a healthy pond environment.
